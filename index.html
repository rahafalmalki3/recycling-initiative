About

This program has been developed to give employees an environmentally responsible way to dispose of used workwear and FRC. Whether it's old, stained, damaged or too small, unwanted Aramco clothing placed in our recycle bins will be reprocessed.

The resulting materials will then be used to create new products, reducing the demand for new fabric and yarn, which require a lot of resources to produce.

Recycling Locations

Drop off your uniforms at the location closest to you and give them a new life through recycling.

Scan or Click the code to find the nearest drop-off point.
